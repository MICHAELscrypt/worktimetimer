Learn more about 25-5

25-5 is a simple and flexible Pomodoro Timer. It can be used as a standalone web app on desktop or phone. But 25-5 can also be used as a widget in OBS for your work or study streams.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a study or work time management method. Your work time is split into work and break sessions. Traditionally a work session lasts 25 minutes and a break session 5 minutes.
